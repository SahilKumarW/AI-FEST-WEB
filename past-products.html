Past Products

Over the years, the RCAI Lab at NED University Karachi has been actively involved in developing innovative autonomous vehicle technologies. Our dedicated team of researchers and engineers has worked on numerous projects, pushing the boundaries of autonomous systems and contributing to advancements in the field. Here are some of our notable past products:

Project 1: Autonomous Delivery Robot

Our team developed an autonomous delivery robot capable of navigating through complex urban environments. The robot was equipped with advanced sensors, computer vision algorithms, and path planning capabilities. It successfully demonstrated its ability to autonomously deliver packages to designated locations, showcasing the potential for efficient and secure last-mile deliveries.

Project 2: Self-Driving Car Prototype

In collaboration with industry partners, we designed and built a self-driving car prototype. The vehicle incorporated state-of-the-art perception systems, machine learning algorithms, and real-time decision-making capabilities. Through extensive testing and refinement, we achieved significant milestones in autonomous driving, including advanced lane keeping, obstacle detection and avoidance, and adaptive cruise control.

Project 3: Intelligent Traffic Management System

Our team developed an intelligent traffic management system that leveraged connected vehicle technology and artificial intelligence. The system aimed to enhance traffic flow, reduce congestion, and improve overall road safety. By utilizing vehicle-to-vehicle and vehicle-to-infrastructure communication, we successfully implemented adaptive signal control algorithms that optimized traffic signal timings based on real-time traffic conditions.
